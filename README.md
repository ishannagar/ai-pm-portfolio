## What I think about AI products

A few things I believe strongly after 11 years in product and 
building AI agents hands-on:

**Prompting is product design.** The system prompt is your spec. 
Bad prompts = bad product. Great prompts = great UX.

**RAG before fine-tuning.** Always. Unless you have 1000+ examples, 
a clear quality gap, and the budget. Most teams don't.

**Workflows before agents.** Deterministic beats autonomous for 
reliability. Add AI judgement only where rules genuinely fail.

**Evals are non-negotiable.** If you can't measure it, you can't 
ship it confidently. LLM-as-judge is underused.

**MCP is the integration layer AI needed.** What n8n does visually, 
MCP does programmatically. Both matter for enterprise.
